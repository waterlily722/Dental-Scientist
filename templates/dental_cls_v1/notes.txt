Dental classification template.

This template is a compact low-resource research sandbox for benchmarked dental image classification.

Focus on ideas that are clinically meaningful, easy to execute, and easy to compare against the baseline.

Recommended idea families:
- quality-aware preprocessing for radiographs
- imbalance-aware training objectives
- calibration and threshold selection for clinically aligned operating points
- robustness under safe image perturbations
- lightweight backbone trade-offs under fixed data and splits

Avoid or treat as high-risk:
- any change that alters benchmark fairness or split integrity
- using external datasets, pseudo-labels, or external foundation models
- label-breaking augmentations or unrealistic geometric transforms
- ideas that require major rewrites of the training stack
- ideas that depend on large compute budgets

A strong contribution is not only a better metric. It can also be a safer operating point, stronger calibration, or a clearer study of what helps on dental radiographs and why.
